Le'Bananacue Recipe

Back to home

Description

Bananacue is a barbaqued banana. Link to original recipe

Ingredients

  • 5-10 saba (Cardaba banana or plantain) peeled
  • brown sugar for coating
  • cooking oil enough for deep frying

Steps

  1. Preheat oil in work or frying pan.
  2. Peel bananas.
  3. Coat with brown sugar.
  4. Add them into hot oil.
  5. Add more brown sugar.
  6. Make sure the sugar sticks to the banana by mixing them constantly.
  7. When cooked, take them one by one and let the oil drip or you can place them on a strainer.
  8. Serve on bar-b-q sticks with about 1-2 bananas per stick optional
